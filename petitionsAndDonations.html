Contributions of Women in Stem

This change.org petition started by Kathryn Ross, a fellow female student in STEM, highlights the necessity to highlight female role models in the field, working to rectify the imbalance in the curriculum. Kathryn believes, “A small step in making the field more inclusive could result in a huge increase in female enrolment and participation.”
