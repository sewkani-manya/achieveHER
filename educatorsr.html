Below you can find resources that help empower women!

The Geena Davis Institute

Includes lesson plans for addressing gender equality in schools and research studies on gender in media.

PBS Parenting

Includes tips and strategies for building confidence, resolving relationship conflicts and challenging stereotypes.

TrueChild

Tools for parents and educators to initiate “gender transformative approaches” that challenge gender norms and inequities.

Teach a Girl to Lead

Includes “teaching toolbox” with lesson plans, activities, and multimedia resources to help young women rethink leadership.

Teaching Tolerance

Includes exercises on challenging stereotypes, female identity, gender expectations, and lessons about influential women throughout history.

Wrestling with Manhood

Amidst the growing popularity of professional wrestling among male youth, Wrestling with Manhood addresses the relationship to real-life violence. This site encourages viewers to think about the enduring problems of men’s violence against women and bullying in our schools.

American Association of University Women (AAUW)

Offers resources and lesson plans on topics related to gender inequality including those on women’s history, suffrage, policies and law, and women in leadership.

National Coalition for Women and Girls in Education (NCWGE)

Updates users on relevant federal education legislation and shares useful resources and publications, dedicated to improving educational opportunities for girls and women.

Girl Ventures

Allows users to discover downloadable and online tools as well as other organizations working toward greater inclusivity and equity for youth in the outdoors
