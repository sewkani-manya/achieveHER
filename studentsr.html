Support

Below you can find resources that work to empower young women and build confidence!

Girls Inc develops informal education programs and educates media about the challenges girls face, teaching girls how to advocate for themselves and their community.

Girls Leadership Girls Leadership works to equip girls with the power to use their voice, emphasizing the power in and necessity of advocacy.

Girls Write Now GWN includes research and strategies to promote awareness of and counter negative stereotypes.
Girls Scout With over 2.8 million members across the world, Girl Scouts conducts research on girls’ development while offering an outlet for girls to discover power through community service, field trips, and cultural exchanges.
Girls on the Run Physical activity based youth development program for girls in 3rd through 8th grade, teaching life skills through interactive lessons and games.
Girls Who Code pave the way for women in tech as “leaders, healers and revolutionaries,” GWC is on a mission to close the gender gap in technology by teaching FREE summer coding programs for young women.
The Representation Project Fighting sexism through films, education, research, and activism.
Tech Girls Offers a list of over 100 sites for girls to get involved in computer science and tech.
Kode with Klossy Creates learning experiences and opportunities for young women that increase their confidence and inspire them to pursue tech for FREE, building a route for future female engineers with no prior experience necessary.
